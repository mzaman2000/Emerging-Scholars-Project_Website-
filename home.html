About Code Cyber Team

Malware such as Ransomware is among the most severe threats in today's digital environment. Most people who study cybersecurity appear to have trouble getting all of the resources they require to become more effective in that profession in today's society. We aim to highlight the relevance of the cyber world and learn what it takes to work in cyber security in the modern digital age. Not only that, but we will investigate and collect information on cyber security, do internet research, meet with cyber security experts, and compile and organize data in an easy-to-read website style. We will also outline Security Protocols and System Vulnerabilities that arise in standard cellphones and internet-connected gadgets (IOT, Internet of Things). We will perform internet research, organize data, build websites, and make connections in the cyber security industry.
